# Equity Research

## Overview

This section of my portfolio contains independent equity research and qualitative business analysis projects completed as part of my learning journey in finance.

The objective of these projects is not to produce investment recommendations, but to develop a structured approach to understanding businesses through management communication, annual reports, earnings calls, financial statements, industry research, and valuation techniques.

As my learning progresses, this repository will expand to include both qualitative and quantitative research across multiple sectors.

---

## Research Philosophy

I believe good investment research combines both numbers and narrative.

While financial models explain *what* happened, management commentary, capital allocation decisions, competitive positioning, and strategic execution often explain *why* it happened.

This repository aims to develop both perspectives.
