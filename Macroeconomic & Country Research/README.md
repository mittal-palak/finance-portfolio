# Macroeconomic & Country Research

This section of my finance portfolio contains independent research on countries, macroeconomic systems, financial markets, and public policy.

The objective is to understand how economic institutions, monetary policy, fiscal frameworks, demographics, geopolitics, and financial systems influence long-term business and investment outcomes.

Unlike equity research, which focuses on individual companies, these projects analyze economies at a country level and examine the broader environment in which businesses operate.

## Research Methodology

Each report is prepared using publicly available information from credible sources, including:

- Government publications
- Central bank reports
- International organizations (IMF, World Bank, OECD)
- Annual reports
- Industry publications
- Research papers
- Financial news and market data

The emphasis is on understanding economic fundamentals rather than making investment recommendations.
