# 🇨🇭 Switzerland: The Financial Powerhouse of the World

An independent macroeconomic research project examining why Switzerland continues to rank among the world's most resilient financial centres despite its relatively small economy.

This report explores the institutional, monetary, fiscal, and financial foundations that have enabled Switzerland to maintain long-term economic stability, global competitiveness, and leadership in private wealth management.

---

## Project Overview

The research analyzes Switzerland through multiple dimensions, including:

- Macroeconomic performance
- Monetary policy and the Swiss National Bank
- Fiscal discipline and public debt
- Banking sector evolution
- Wealth management ecosystem
- Capital markets
- Trade competitiveness
- Comparative analysis with other financial centres

The objective is to understand the structural factors behind Switzerland's sustained financial strength rather than relying solely on economic indicators.

## Research Methodology

This project is based entirely on secondary research using publicly available institutional data.

### Sources include

- Swiss National Bank (SNB)
- International Monetary Fund (IMF)
- World Bank
- OECD
- UBS Annual Reports
- SIX Swiss Exchange
- Swiss Federal Government Publications

Data from these sources was consolidated, cleaned, and visualized in Microsoft Excel before being incorporated into the report.
